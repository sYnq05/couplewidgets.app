Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

🔐 Short version: CoupleWidgets is designed for privacy. We only collect the data strictly necessary to make the app work. Your location and messages are only shared with your connected partner – never with third parties or advertisers.

1. Who we are

CoupleWidgets ("we", "us", "our") is an iOS application developed independently. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at jakob-julius@web.de.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary for the app’s core features to function:

  • Location data: Your approximate or precise location is used to calculate and display the distance between you and your partner. Location data is shared only with your connected partner and is not stored on our servers beyond the time needed to transmit it.
  • Messages: Short text messages you send through the app are transmitted to your partner’s device and may be temporarily stored to enable delivery.
  • Countdown events: Dates and labels for countdown timers you create are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your partner’s device.
  • Partner pairing data: A random unique identifier is created to link your account with your partner’s device. This does not include your name or email address unless you provide them voluntarily.

3. What data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifier by default.
  • We do not collect payment or financial data.
  • We do not use advertising networks or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that track your behavior across apps.

4. How we use your data

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Displaying the real-time distance between you and your partner on your widgets.
  • Delivering messages you choose to send to your partner’s lock screen.
  • Synchronizing countdown events between paired devices.
  • Maintaining the pairing connection between your device and your partner’s device.

5. Data sharing

Your data is shared only with your connected partner. We do not share your data with any third parties, except where required by applicable law. If you use third-party services integrated into your iOS device (e.g., iCloud for backups), their respective privacy policies apply.

6. Data retention

Location data is not permanently stored. Messages are retained only as long as necessary for delivery. If you delete your account or uninstall the app, your data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your data. You can also delete your data directly within the app.
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jakob-julius@web.de.

8. Data deletion in the app

If CoupleWidgets allows you to create an account, you can initiate account deletion directly within the app at any time by navigating to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your data from our systems.

9. Children’s privacy

CoupleWidgets is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Location permissions

CoupleWidgets requires access to your device’s location to display the distance widget. You can control location permissions at any time in your iPhone’s Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → CoupleWidgets. If you disable location access, the distance widget will not function, but all other features remain available.

11. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmitted between devices is encrypted in transit.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:
