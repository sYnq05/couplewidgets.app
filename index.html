Features

Everything you need to feel connected

Three beautiful lock screen widgets, designed for couples who want to stay close – wherever they are.

📍
Live Distance Widget
Always know how far away your partner is. The distance widget updates automatically and displays the current distance right on your lock screen – no app opening needed.
💌
Lock Screen Messages
Send sweet notes that appear directly on your partner’s lock screen. A little reminder that you’re thinking of them – always visible, always personal.
⏳
Countdown Widget
Create a countdown to the moments that matter most – your next date, anniversary, or reunion. Watch the days tick down together, side by side on your screens.
🔒
Lock Screen Native
Built natively for iOS 16+ lock screen widgets. Seamlessly integrated into the iOS experience you already love, with beautiful design that matches any wallpaper.
🎨
Customizable Style
Choose from multiple widget sizes and color styles. Make your lock screen truly yours – and your partner’s. Mix and match to find the perfect look.
🔐
Private by Design
Your messages and location are only shared with your partner – nobody else. We take your privacy seriously and collect the minimum data possible.
How it works

Set up in minutes

Getting started with CoupleWidgets is quick and simple.

1

Download the app

Get CoupleWidgets from the App Store on both your and your partner’s iPhone.

2

Connect with your partner

Share a simple invite link or code to pair your devices together securely.

3

Add your widgets

Long-press your lock screen, tap the + button, and add your CoupleWidgets.

4

Stay connected

Send messages, see your distance, and count down to your next moment – always within reach.

🔐

Your privacy matters

CoupleWidgets is built with privacy at its core. Your location and messages are only ever shared with your connected partner – never with third parties, advertisers, or anyone else. We collect the minimum data required to make the app work, and you can delete your data at any time.
