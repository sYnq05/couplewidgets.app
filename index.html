Features

Everything you need to feel connected

Four beautiful widgets for your lock screen, designed for couples who want to stay close – wherever they are.

📍
Live Distance Widget
Always know how far away your partner is. The distance widget updates automatically using your location and displays the current distance right on your lock screen – no app opening needed.
💌
Shared Note Widget
You and your partner share one note (up to 100 characters) that appears directly on your lock screens. Write a sweet message, update it any time, and your partner gets a notification instantly.
⏳
Countdown Widget
Create a countdown to the moments that matter most – your next date, anniversary, or reunion. Watch the days tick down together, side by side on your screens.
🔥
Streak Widget
Keep your connection alive every day. The streak widget tracks consecutive days of activity between you and your partner, celebrating how long you've stayed in touch.
🔒
Lock Screen Native
Built natively for iOS 16+ lock screen widgets. Seamlessly integrated into the iOS experience you already love, with beautiful design that matches any wallpaper.
🔐
Private by Design
Sign in securely with your Apple ID. Your note and location are only shared with your connected partner – never with third parties or advertisers. Sync is powered by Apple's iCloud.
How it works

Set up in minutes

Getting started with TwoUfUs is quick and simple.

1

Download the app

Get TwoUfUs from the App Store on both your and your partner's iPhone (iOS 16+).

2

Sign in with Apple

Create your account securely using Sign in with Apple – no password, no separate registration required.

3

Connect with your partner

Share your unique invite code with your partner. Once they enter it, you're paired and ready to go.

4

Add your widgets

Long-press your lock screen, tap the + button, and add your TwoUfUs widgets. Stay connected, always.

🔐

Your privacy matters

TwoUfUs is built with privacy at its core. Your location and notes are only ever shared with your connected partner – never with third parties, advertisers, or anyone else. We use Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) for sync, so your data stays within Apple's secure infrastructure. Sign in with Apple means no separate account, no password to manage.
